Description

Nothing like oatmeal cookies this Fall!

Ingredients for a dozen cookies:

  1. 4 eggs
  2. oatmeal
  3. butter
  4. 2 tbs vanella extract
  5. brown sugar (to your liking)
  6. sugar (to your liking)
  7. cinnamon
  8. 1 tsp sea salt

Directions

  • Add eggs and vanilla extract and beat for 60 seconds
  • Add brown sugar, dry ingredients
  • Add oatmeal
  • Add any additional mix-ins that you prefer (raisins,bananas, etc. )
  • roll into your prefered size
  • place cookies on parchment papaer or oiled pan and bake for 15 minutes
  • Once the timer sets off, it's time to enjoy!
