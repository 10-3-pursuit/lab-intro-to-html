Quiche Recipe

Home Sundial

Simple quiche recipe that can easily be customized

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • pie crust
  • shredded cheese (swiss or cheddar)
  • 1 1/2 cup half and half milk
  • any combination of meat and/or vegetables

Directions

  1. Dice your vegetables and/or meats.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  3. Whisk your eggs and milk together in a bowl. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. To your pie crust, add your cheese and fillings.
  5. Fill your pie with the egg mixture, making sure no cheese is sticking out.
  6. Season the top with paprika and put in the oven for about 40 minutes.
