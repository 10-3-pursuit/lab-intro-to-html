The Perfect Grilled Cheese

Description

This grilled cheese sandwich is the perfect combination of melted cheese, crispy bread, fresh tomatoes, and buttery goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • salt
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese
  • butter

Directions

  1. Generouly spread butter on both sides of each slice of bread.
  2. Add bread slices to hot pan.
  3. Let the bread slices toast on one side to a golden brown, then flip over.
  4. Add cheese to toasted side of bread.
  5. Cover pan until cheese it melted.
  6. Add tomatoes with some salt (to taste).
  7. Remove from pan and cut sandwich in half diagonally.
  8. Enjoy!
