The Perfect Smoothie

Description

This smoothie will make you want to put your nephew in a leg lock!

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 6 Strawberries
  • 1 Cup of Blueberries
  • 3 Medjool Dates
  • 2 Spoons of Peanut Butter
  • 1 1/2 Cups of Milk
  • 2 Teaspoons of cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 Cups of Ice

Directions

  1. Prepare your Ingredients
  2. Plug in your blender
  3. Add all the Ingredients to the blender
  4. Pour the smoothie into a glass
  5. Enjoy!
