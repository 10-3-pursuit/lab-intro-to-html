Grilled Cheese

Description

You're never going to want to eat anything else after learning how to make this bomb sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of texas toast
  • Unsalted butter
  • American cheese

Directions

  1. Spread 1/2 Tbsp butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place a skillet over low to medium/low heat. A griddle should be at 275ËšF. Immediately add 2 slices of bread with the butter-side-down.
  3. Stack cheeses on one of the pieces of bread, cover with the other piece of toast and flip the sandwich over.
  4. Continue sauteeing, flipping once, until both sides are golden brown and cheese is melted.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half on the diagonal to serve.
