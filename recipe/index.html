The Perfect Sandwich

Description

A roast beef sandwich is the best kind of sandwich, hands down. Here's a recipe for how to make a great one!

Ingredients

  • Your choice of bread (preferably a sub or kaiser roll)
  • 3 slices of cheese
  • 6 cuts of roast beef
  • Mayo
  • 1/2 cup of chopped lettuce
  • 3 slices of tomato

Equipment/Tableware

  • A toaster or oven
  • A butter knife
  • A plate

Directions

  1. Adjust ingredients base don personal preference and bread size
  2. Toast the bread
  3. Spread mayo on to bread
  4. (Alternatively, you could warm the meat and cheese on a grill, then place on the sandwich, thus skipping steps 5-11)
  5. Place two pieces of meat on one slice of the bread
  6. Place one piece of cheese on the opposite slice
  7. Place one piece of cheese on the slice with meat on top
  8. Place two pieces of meat on the slice with just cheese
  9. Place one piece of cheese on the slice with meat on top
  10. Place two pieces of meat on the oposite slice
  11. Toast sandwich
  12. Add lettuce and tomato to sandwich
  13. Enjoy!
