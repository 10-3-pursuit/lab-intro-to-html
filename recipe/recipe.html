Pelau Made Simple!

Your alternative recipe for pelau

Description

One of the many national dishes of TnT, Pelau is rice medley dish similar to a Spanish paella or jambayala.

Ingredients

  • white rice
  • pigeon peas
  • brown sugar
  • peppa sauce
  • shadow bennee
  • salt
  • onions
  • carrots
  • coconut milk

Directions

  1. boil hot water
  2. add 2 table spoons of brown sugar
  3. burn the pot
  4. dump all ingredients in
  5. stir in for 15mins then cover
  6. leave for 25mins til moist!
