The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of wheat bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a CLEAN plate.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on the bottom.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Enjoy your delectable sandwich now with a tall glass of iced cold Almond Milk or Oat Milk!🤪
