Cheesecake



Description

This classic cheesecake is rich, creamy, and absolutely delicious. It's a perfect treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
  • 3 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch (23cm) springform pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, beating well with each addition. Stir in sour cream and flour until smooth.
  4. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the pan.
  5. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until the center is almost set. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake in the oven for 1 hour.
  6. Remove from the oven and cool completely on a wire rack. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
