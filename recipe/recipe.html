GRiLLED CHEESE

Go BACK

Description

A simple and easy to make sandwich any cheese lover can enjoy.

Ingredients

*Bread
*Butter(optional)
*Cheese(Any cheese)

Directions

1. Make sure your pan is hot
2. If using butter add butter to pan if not skip this step
3. Add cheese between two slice of bread, close the sandwich then place sandwich on stove
4.Wait till one side of bread is toasted then flip(Repeat till satifised with color of bread)
5.Remove from pan and plate your grilled cheese sandwich
6.Enjoy!
